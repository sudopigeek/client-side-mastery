Tennessee's 5th Congressional District Representative

Jim Cooper

Cooper is the representative for Tennessee’s 5th congressional district (view map) and is a Democrat. He has served since Jan 7, 2003. Cooper is next up for reelection in 2020. He was previously the representative for Tennessee’s 4th congressional district as a Democrat from 1983 to 1994.

Jim Cooper sits on the following committees:

  • House Committee on Armed Forces
  • Strategic Forces
  • Intelligence and Projection Forces
  • Seapower and Projection Forces
  • House Committee on the Budget
  • House Committee on Oversight and Reform
  • National Security

Cooper was the primary sponsor of 5 bils that were enacted:

  • H. R. 482 (112th): Water Heater Rating Improvment Act of 2011
  • H. R. 928 (110th): Inspector General Reform Act of 2008
  • H. J. Res. 297 (103rd): To designate 1994 as "The Year of Gospel Music"
  • H. R. 5667 (101st): To amend the Water Resources Development Act
  • H. R. 971 (101st): Telphone Operator Consumer Services Improvement Act of 1990
